Design Brief


(1) What is the purpose (goal) of the design?

The goal of the design is to attract attention to the Activate! 2020 Exhibition.


(2) Who is the audience?

The target audience is majority college-aged students from 18-25 years old.


(3) What message needs to be communicated?

The message that needs to be communicated is the urgency, community, and temporary nature of the exhibition.


(4) What is the competition and marketplace?

(How the competition has solved similar problems, list 5 websites)


(5) What is the context?

(Where, how and when a design will be seen. Environment, circumstance)


(6) In what voice?

(01: Personality or expressive voice. Write one sentence to describe the topic) (02: List 5 adjectives to describe it.)


(7) What kind of response is desired

(If you can get the audience to react, to feel, to purchase, to think...)
