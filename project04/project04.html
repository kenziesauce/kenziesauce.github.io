Design Brief

1. Who is Makenzie?

Makenzie is a person who hopes to use her passion of design to build community designing informative and accessible work for nonprofits and charities related to the musical instrument business.

2. What is my Design Purpose?

Design should be accessible and meaningful. Design should be fun. Design should call people to action.

3. Who is my Target Audience?

Target audience are individuals who want to uplift the world around them through fun and meaningful design.

4. Who is my Competition?

Create a website splashpage for your website

5. 7 Adjectives to Describe my Design?

Happy Curious Imaginative Goofy Musical Fun Soothing

6. How do I want People to Respond?

I want a positive and excited response.

7. Why am I creating this Website?

I am creating this website to show off my hard work and bring my creative skills into the public.
