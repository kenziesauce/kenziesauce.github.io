Extention and International Program Marketing Campaign

Extension & International Programs (EIP) contracted a four month long multimedia campaign consisting of weekly digital and print ads for their intersession programs such as winter courses.

Campaign successfully raised Winter Session enrollment by 13% and won Second Place Best Print Advertisement at the California College Media Association awards & Second Place Best Multimedia Ad Campaign at the College Media Business & Advertising Managers awards.
