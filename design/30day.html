30 Days of Musical Motion   

Created a two to six-second looping motion graphic animation of a different percussion instrument every day for 30 days. Style inspiration for the project came from the flat iconography of Russian Constructivists and the bold visual movement of Swiss Design.

The idea for this project came from my love of making music and curiosity about motion graphic techniques. I see so many impressive explorations of motion on my Instagram and I have been aching to explore that realm of design. I wanted to challenge myself to express how I feel about music through the medium.
